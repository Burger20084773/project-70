Campus life

School Library

Was estabilished in 2009

Cambridge school's library has many books to offer along with a quiet environment for kids to study in. It features not only educational books but two sections completely dedicated to the fun books that all kids enjoy reading. It has books about everything. to this day it still is a very great library



School field

Was built in 2009

The school field is one of the many highlights of cambridge school.kids can play all sorts of sports and games. it has a section for basketball and soccer. the school sports day is held here and the large area can accomodate upto 2000 people.



School Auditorium

Was built in 2010

The school auditorium is an ernomous space where the students of cambridge school display their many talents. it is also where the morning assembly for the school is conducted. It can hold upto 1000 people and is a very beautiful place



New AV Room

We have a new Av room that teachers use to show all the amazing videos we need to study with. On special days we get to watch cool movies.With this room students have started to enjoy their learning process a bit more.
